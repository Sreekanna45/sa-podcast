Episode 01: Inspiring SPEC Journey

ft. Ms. K. Priyaswi — 21st May 2025

Guest: Ms. Priyaswi shares her memories, challenges, and inspiring moments from SPEC.

In the "Bites and Banter" podcast, departing professor Whiskey Priyashi reflects on her journey as an empathetic educator and mentor. She discusses her philosophy of building trust through communication over strict rules, shares unique stress-management techniques, and offers heartfelt insights on student life, leadership, and the value of perseverance.
